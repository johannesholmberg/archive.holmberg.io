A bit about myself

8 years experience of designing and developing usable online solutions. What I do is making design and technology work together on the web.

My services include interaction design, information architecture and front-end development. I work together with teams from companies all over the world to provide responsive and optimized user interfaces.

I’ve worked on products together with teams from Valtech, Knowit and Culturalcare’s in-house team in India. If you want to know more about my experience, you should check out my cv or my work.

Personal

When not sitting in front of the screen trying to make these puzzle pieces fit, I am usually out hiking somewhere in the forest. It gives some peace. And inspiration.

About this website

This website was made for the purpose of creating a digital home to collect the writing and the work I do. The last couple of years I have downsized on my social media appearance and wanted to have a simple home where I can experiment at my own pace.

It’s built on Jekyll and hosted at MediaTemple. All CSS is written using Sass. The task manager I use is Gulp and Bower helps me keep track of packages. Icons come from the Streamline icon library. Photos are taken by Erik Carlsson. Questions? johannes@holmberg.io
