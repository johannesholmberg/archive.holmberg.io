Colophon

The website was made for the purpose of creating a digital home to collect the writing and the work I do. The last couple of years I have downsized on my social media appearance and wanted to have a simple home where I can experiment at my own pace. The site is hand coded with HTML5 and CSS3. It’s built on top of Jekyll. The CSS is written with Sass and minification stuff and other automations are happening with Gulp. I also use Bower to keep a good overview of the packages I use. It’s currently hosted at MediaTemple.The photo on the start page is taken by Erik Carlsson.

I see this website as my playground so I don’t always worry how it looks across various browsers. But I’m keen about accessibility and usability issues so if you find something I might have missed, please let me know.
