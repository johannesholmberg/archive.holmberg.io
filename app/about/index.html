This website

The website was made for the purpose of creating a digital home to collect the writing and the work I do. The last couple of years I have downsized on my social media appearance and wanted to have a simple home where I can experiment at my own pace.

  • Designed and coded from the ground up by yours truly.

  • CSS is compiled with Sass.
  • Task manager is Gulp.
